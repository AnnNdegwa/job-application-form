Movie Review:You Again

You Again (2019) is a Kenyan Romantic Comedy directed by and starring Nick Mutuma, marking his directorial debut. The film follows Sophia and Kingsley(Nick Mutuma), ex-lovers paired as news-anchors years after a painful breakup. The movie was described as a hit romantic comedy that captures modern Kenyan relationships.

Movie Rating⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆(9.5/10)
